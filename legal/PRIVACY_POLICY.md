# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

**GetLifeStats** ("we", "us", or "our") operates the getlifestats.com website (the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
When you use our Service, you may voluntarily provide:
- **Birthday/Date of Birth** — Used to calculate life statistics
- **Gender** — Used to provide gender-specific statistics (e.g., life expectancy)
- **Country** — Used to provide location-specific statistics
- **Lifestyle Information** — Height, weight, activity level, sleep habits, work patterns (if you choose to personalize your experience)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** require account creation
- We do **not** collect your name, email, or contact information
- We do **not** use cookies for tracking
- We do **not** sell your personal information

### Automatically Collected Information
We may collect standard web analytics data:
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referring website

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:
1. Calculate and display your personalized life statistics
2. Improve the Service
3. Generate anonymized, aggregate statistics (never individual data)

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your preferences may be stored in your browser's local storage for convenience
- **Database Storage**: If you choose to save your profile, data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption
- **Retention**: You may delete your data at any time

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share **anonymized, aggregate data** (e.g., "users in the US average 7 hours of sleep") for:
- Research purposes
- Improving the Service
- Business analytics

**Anonymized data cannot be traced back to any individual.**

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data
- **Export** your data
- **Opt-out** of any data collection

## Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
